Description

Serves 2. This spicy tuna recipe combines tender chunks of fresh tuna with creamy mayo, sriracha, and a hint of soy sauce for a perfect balance of heat and flavor. The mixture is brightened with a touch of lime and green onions, creating a bold, zesty kick. Serve it over rice, in sushi rolls, or on crispy wontons for a fiery, satisfying bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of rice
  • Fresh Tuna
  • 2 tablespoons soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1/2 cup Mayo
  • 1 cup Cream
  • 1 Lime
  • 1/4 cup chopped Green Onions

Steps

  1. Make white rice via instructions from the back of the rice package.
  2. Chop up fresh tuna into bite size cubes.
  3. In a seperate bowl mix, mayo, sriracha, and soy sauce together.
  4. Mix the tuna with the spicy mayo recipe.
  5. While letting this mixture sit, shape rice into 10 small rectangles and place on pan.
  6. Place rice into air fryer for 10 minutes
  7. Remove rice from air fryer and top with spicy tuna mix.
  8. Garnish with green onions and lime. Enjoy!

Description

Serves 3-4. This miso soup recipe features a warm, savory broth made from dashi and miso paste, creating a comforting and flavorful base. Soft tofu cubes and sliced green onions add texture and freshness to each spoonful. Simple yet satisfying, it‚Äôs a quick and nourishing dish perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Chicken Broth
  • 2 tablespoon Miso Paste
  • 1 cup dashi
  • 3 Tofu Cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped Green Onions
  • Salt (preference)
  • Pepper (preference)

Steps

  1. Pour chicken broth and dashi into large pot and heat until simmering.
  2. Once simmering, mix in miso paste and stir.
  3. Add tofu cubes.
  4. Let sit on low heat for about 15 minutes.
  5. Top with green onions and salt or pepper to your likeing
  6. Enjoy
