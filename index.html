Spicy Tuna Crispy Rice

Description

This spicy tuna recipe combines tender chunks of fresh tuna with creamy mayo, sriracha, and a hint of soy sauce for a perfect balance of heat and flavor. The mixture is brightened with a touch of lime and green onions, creating a bold, zesty kick. Serve it over rice, in sushi rolls, or on crispy wontons for a fiery, satisfying bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of rice
  • Fresh Tuna
  • 2 tablespoons soy Sauce
  • Sriracha
  • Mayo
  • Cream
  • Lime
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Make white rice via instructions from the back of the rice package.
  2. Chop up fresh tuna into bite size cubes.
  3. In a seperate bowl mix, mayo, sriracha, and soy sauce together.
  4. Mix the tuna with the spicy mayo recipe.
  5. While letting this mixture sit, shape rice into 10 small rectangles and place on pan.
  6. Place rice into air fryer for 10 minutes
  7. Remove rice from air fryer and top with spicy tuna mix.
  8. Garnish with green onions and lime. Enjoy!
